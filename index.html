HELLO!

My name is Cassandra, graphic designer here. I love bands, books, and all things art! This is my website, let’s you know what my style is and what i’m capable of doing. After starting my journey in College for Graphic Design I wanted to learn and expand my knowledge in print work, UI/UX design and packaging, now here I am, learning new things everyday! MY STUFF

FESTIPASS

Festipass is an app that let's you get into your favourite music festivals and also find your friends easily while on site.

BANDMATE

BANDMATE is an app for state champs everything you need to know and see about them. It acts like an information app to check out the latest photos, music, tour dates and their social media information as well.

MIRROR MIRROR

Mirror Mirror is a virtual change room that enables shoppers to buy clothing more easily. It is linked to an app that controlssaid smart mirror.
